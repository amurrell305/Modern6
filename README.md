# Modern6
A new, revitalized, and modern iOS icon pack reminiscent of iOS 6.

## What is Modern6?
Modern6 is a new take on the classic and nostalgic look of iOS 6. Modern6 will revitalize every system app icon, from the ground up. Included will also be icons for other popular apps, settings, and even the status bar. Everything will be compatible with Snowboard on at least iOS 12.5.7. As I am only one person with only one Apple device on one iOS version, that is what I will optimize the theme for.
